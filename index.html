RxVision

A visualizer / debugger for reactive streams

This is an example chart, generated from a simple program that generates "who to follow" recommendations from github (from the excellent tutorial by @staltz).

If you want to look at the code, it's in a gist at the moment.

Try mousing over things.

  • light blue: produced here
  • dark blue: consumed here
  • large gray: not passed anywhere
  • dotted pink line: async transition
